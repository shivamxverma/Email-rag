# Sample questions and expected citations

These examples are for **thread T-001** (General correspondence & forecasts). Each question should be answered from the selected thread only; expected citations are message IDs that should appear in the answer.

Format:
- **Email:** `[msg: <message_id>]`
- **Attachment (when applicable):** `[msg: <message_id>, page: <n>]`

---

1. **What was discussed about the North of Stanfield forecast for January?**  
   - **Expect:** Answer mentions supply/demand, figures (e.g. Sumas, Roosevelt), and a date or month.  
   - **Must cite:** The message(s) containing the Jan forecast (e.g. the excerpt that includes "North of Stanfield forecast for Jan", "Supply", "Demand", "Total Supply 1325").  
   - **Example citation:** `[msg: <message_id>]` for the email with that forecast table.

2. **Who sent the forecast and to whom?**  
   - **Expect:** Sender and recipient (e.g. Phillip Allen, John/Jonathan).  
   - **Must cite:** The message(s) that state From/To.  
   - **Example citation:** `[msg: <message_id>]`.

3. **What is Roosevelt capacity?**  
   - **Expect:** A number (e.g. 495).  
   - **Must cite:** The message where Roosevelt capacity is stated.  
   - **Example citation:** `[msg: <message_id>]`.

4. **What did the sender ask the recipient to do or compare?**  
   - **Expect:** Request such as “let me know how your forecast differs” or similar.  
   - **Must cite:** The message containing that request.  
   - **Example citation:** `[msg: <message_id>]`.

5. **Which months or time periods appear in the forecasts?**  
   - **Expect:** At least Jan, possibly Dec, YoY comparison.  
   - **Must cite:** Message(s) that list the months/columns.  
   - **Example citation:** `[msg: <message_id>]`.

6. **Summarize the main numbers for Total Supply and Total Demand in the thread.**  
   - **Expect:** Numbers that match the forecast table (e.g. 1325, 1241, 1371).  
   - **Must cite:** The message(s) containing the table.  
   - **Example citation:** `[msg: <message_id>]`.

7. **When was the forecast sent (date or relative time)?**  
   - **Expect:** A date or “January” / “Dec” etc.  
   - **Must cite:** The message with the Date header or date in body.  
   - **Example citation:** `[msg: <message_id>]`.

8. **What locations or regions are mentioned (e.g. North of Chehalis, Sumas)?**  
   - **Expect:** Named locations from the forecast.  
   - **Must cite:** The message(s) that list them.  
   - **Example citation:** `[msg: <message_id>]`.

9. **Is there any mention of capacity or limits besides Roosevelt?**  
   - **Expect:** Either “no” or a short quote if present.  
   - **Must cite:** Only if such mention exists.  
   - **Example citation:** `[msg: <message_id>]` if applicable.

10. **What was the tone or purpose of the message that included the forecast table?**  
    - **Expect:** Informational, request for comparison, etc.  
    - **Must cite:** The message in question.  
    - **Example citation:** `[msg: <message_id>]`.
